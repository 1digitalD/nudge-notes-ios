# Privacy Policy Draft

`nudge Notes` stores your wellness data on your device by default. Daily logs, WHR measurements, notes, goals, and optional photos remain local unless you explicitly export them.

We request photo library access only when you choose to attach or export photos. We request notification access only if you want reminder notifications. These permissions are optional and can be revoked in iOS Settings at any time.

If you purchase `nudge Notes Pro`, Apple processes the subscription transaction. We do not receive your full payment details. Subscription status is determined through Apple's StoreKit entitlements.

CSV export is only created when you ask for it. Exported files are shared using the standard iOS share sheet and are handled by the apps or destinations you choose.

We do not sell personal data and we do not use third-party advertising SDKs in the app.

For support or privacy questions, publish a contact address before App Store submission and replace this draft with the final hosted policy.
